Hello! We are Brian, Max, Martjin and Dennis.
Thanks for stopping by,

Like all good innovations we needed to solve a problem close to home. Namely, one of us used to sweat a lot.

He had tried every remedy you can imagine yet remained frustrated as none of them worked. The slightest exertion would result in large sweat patches.

T-shirts with built-in panty liners for underarms were tried. Deodorants were sprayed more in hope than in expectation, often to mask the odour rather than prevent sweats.

The final straw was a hot summer’s day. We knew we had to act.

So, we founded The Polar Company together. Our mission is to keep us, and you, cool in every situation!

Martijn & Dennis
